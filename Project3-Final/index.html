What has been the impact of the Paris Agreement?

In 2015 over 190 countries agreed to a legally binding international agreement, called the Paris Agreement.

  • One main goal of the agreement is by 2030 to have reduced 2010 CO2 emissions levels by at least 45%.
  • The graph represents progress towards the 2030 goal from the top CO2 emitters.
  • Only the United States, Japan, Germany, and the United Kingdom have made progress towards the 2030 emissions goal.

    • Additional information can be found at: https://www.un.org/en/climatechange/paris-agreement
